# Competitive Analysis: Auto Repair Shops in Missoula, Montana

**Date:** 2026-02-12  
**Prepared for:** Auto Assist Co. (autoassistco.com)

## Executive Summary

The auto repair market in Missoula, Montana is highly competitive with several well-established players offering comprehensive automotive services. The market is dominated by businesses with strong Google ratings (4.8-5.0 stars) and substantial review volumes. ASAP Automotive Repair leads with perfect 5.0 ratings and 152 reviews, while Meineke dominates with 4.8 stars but a substantial 738 reviews. Auto Assist Co. operates in a premium segment with strong positioning around transparency and local expertise, competing effectively against both franchise operations and independent shops.
