5. **Relatability** – Keeping it real and accessible to all skill levels

## Brand Voice
### Characteristics
1. **Unfiltered & Real** – No fluff, no corporate jargon—just straight talk
2. **Empowering & Action-Driven** – Push people to improve and take action
3. **Sharp-Witted & Thought-Provoking** – Mix of dry humor, sharp insights, and hard truths

### Tone & Language
- **Formality Level**: 1/10 – Fully casual, conversational, and raw
- **Humor Style**: Dry, dark, self-deprecating—but always strategic
- **Do's**: Speak naturally, use off-color language when it fits, motivate and challenge
- **Don'ts**: Sound overly polished, corporate, pretentious, or passive

### What Your Brand Is and Isn't
- ✅ **Is**: Authentic, empowering, knowledgeable, real, engaging
- ❌ **Isn't**: Arrogant, disconnected, uninspiring, uninformed, overly corporate
